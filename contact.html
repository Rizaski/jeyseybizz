Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to complete an order?

Standard orders typically take 5-7 business days, while bulk orders may take 7-14 business days depending on the quantity and complexity.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for specific shipping information to your country.

What file formats do you accept for custom designs?

We accept various formats including PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, and EPS. High-resolution files are preferred for the best print quality.

Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?

Absolutely! We encourage customers to request samples to ensure quality and design accuracy before proceeding with bulk orders.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, contact us for a return or exchange.
